Work

My work varies between Street Photography, Landscape and Portrait.

I am fan of film, that is why I like to produce styles that move out from digital. The texture that comes with film expresses in a better form the moment that was captured through the sensor of the camera.

I have a variety of lenses and accessories (take a look) to use that will simply change in many ways how the same moment is captured, producing different feelings and looks to it.

The lifecycle of my work is as follows:

  • We meet to understand the type of photos/videos you are looking for
  • We agree on a date/time for a session
  • We meet and shoot
  • We work together on the post-processing of the captured images/videos. This is very important as you will define the texture and colors (looks and feels) you like.

About

I am a guy that loves to travel, observe, and capture moments in life with my camera, both still or motion pictures. I like to meet people, listen to their stories, and share my work with others. Music is not behind, I (try to) play the piano and guitar.

Photography becomes a hobbie that allows me to connect with others whilst giving me an opportunity to create an impact through the use of colours and textures.
