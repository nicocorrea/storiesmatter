Work

My focus is Street Photography, Landscape, Portraits, and Ads.

I am a fan of film, because I believe it produces the texture and colors that better convey emotions. Same as those used in Motion Picture.

That is why I use a variety of lenses, accessories and software (take a look) that allows to capture and post-process the stills/motions producing different feelings and looks to it.

The lifecycle of my work is as follows:

  • We meet to understand the type of photos/videos you are looking for
  • We agree on a date/time for a session
  • We meet and shoot
  • We work together during post-processing. This is very important as you will define the texture and colors (looks and feels) you like.

About

I love to travel, explore, and capture moments with my camera (still or motions). I like to meet people, listen to their stories, and share my work with others. Music is not behind, I (try to) play the piano and guitar.

Photography became a hobby that allowed me to connect with others whilst giving me an opportunity to create an impact through the use of colors and textures.
